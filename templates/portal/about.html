About Campus Connect

A simple student support portal for campus communication and issue reporting.

Campus Connect is a simple student support portal designed to make campus issue reporting easier. Students can report lost or found items, raise classroom or IT issues, and follow the progress of their reports in one place.

The project helps reduce confusion by keeping lost/found reports, helpdesk requests, comments, uploaded documents, and status updates organised in a single system. Instead of asking different people or checking multiple places, students can search reports, view updates, and stay informed through announcements.

Staff and admin users can review reports, update statuses, and post announcements so users know what action has been taken.
